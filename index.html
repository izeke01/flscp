Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle regulatory approvals (LASEPA/NESREA)?

Yes. We ensure all our treatment plant designs meet the effluent discharge standards set by NESREA and LASEPA. We assist in preparing the necessary environmental audit reports for approval.

Can you fix existing treatment plants that are not working?

Absolutely. A large part of our business is "Plant Optimization." We audit your failed system, identify the design or chemical flaw, and retrofit it to work correctly.

Do you sell water treatment chemicals?

Yes, we supply industrial-grade chemicals including coagulants, flocculants, chlorine, anti-scalants, and pH adjusters.

What is the cost of a water analysis?

Costs vary depending on the parameters needed (e.g., simple physical tests vs. heavy metal spectrometry). Please contact us for a specific price list.
